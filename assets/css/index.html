About STK

STK (Share Trends Korea) is a professional company that provides information on Korea’s "Beauty and Health" and is spearheading the spread of beauty and health culture originated from Korea around the world.

Humans naturally invest in themselves when their concerns about food and housing are resolved, especially spending money on their appearance and health. Consumption like this has been a natural phenomenon since long ago when the market economy was formed. Recently, K-pop and K-Culture have made many foreigners follow Korean celebrities as they encounter Korean culture, and the Korean medical market (plastic surgery and medical checkups) is already known around the world, and many foreigners are coming to Korea to get the related medical services. However, many damage cases of extravagant expenses have occurred due to treatment by unverified medical staff at unverified medical facilities introduced by medical brokers.

To address this problem, STK is preparing to open the "Meplace" service within the first quarter of 2020 to provide information on proven medical facilities and medical staff so that customers can receive the best beauty and health service. Besides, the company aims to provide Total Care Service by offering One-Stop Service (consultation, therapy and surgery, care, travel, follow-up, etc.) for the promotion of the global medical tour in 2022.

STK will work with organizations and experts from all sectors to become a company that strives to create a reliable medical care service culture.
